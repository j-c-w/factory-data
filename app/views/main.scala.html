The Ready-made Garment Productivity Project (rmgpp.org) aims to collect detailed and comparable productivity data from garment manufacturers in several countries. Initial work has focused primarily on Bangladesh.

The portal allows users to create reports from daily, line-level sewing production data. At present, the data come from 17 factories in Bangladesh that are all suppliers to large well-know brands. Click on the link above to create customized graphs and tables using the data.

Why focus on the garment sector? Garments have often been one of the first steps onto the manufacturing ladder for low-income countries, and an important point of entry into international markets. But the data are also highly comparable across factories because availability of Standard Minute Values allows some comparison even when production lines produce different products.
